                About Me



TL;DR

An ambitious and driven software engineer seeking a challenging and rewarding opportunity to use and grow my tech skills.

TS;WM - Too Short; Want More

I graduated with a Bachelors in Finance from the University of Georgia but never could get truly interested in the subject.  One day I read an article claiming I could learn to be a developer in 6 months so I decided to take the plunge.  5 years and countless articles, tutorials, courses, books, projects and stack overflow questions later I feel like I am ready for a tech career.

I am most confident with Python but I have dabbled with numerous other languages and technologies throughout my journey.   Recently I have become fascinated with the cloud.   So far I have earned the AWS Solutions Architect Associate certification and I am currently studying for the AWS developer certification.

When I am not learning and practicing new tech skills I enjoy golfing, poker, and spending time with my wife and 4 kids.
